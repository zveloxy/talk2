Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really anonymous?

Yes. We do not require any personal information. Your IP is logged briefly for connection handling but is never associated with a persistent profile.

Can I retrieve deleted messages?

No. Once deleted by you or the auto-cleaner, they are physically removed from the storage and cannot be recovered.

Is there a mobile app?

Talk2 works great on mobile browsers. You can add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

Who runs this?

This is an open-source project developed by Veloxy, focused on privacy rights.
